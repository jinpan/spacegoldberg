Going Rogue

You have successfully escaped the Solar System! Your engineers have your bender ready for action, and you plot a course for the nearest star system with a habitable planet.

Halfway through your bend, your spaceship begins to shake, and then the stars come back into focus. You see a strange planet, dead ahead. Turns out that your path wasn’t as clear as you thought it was. In your way is a “rogue planet” - a planet that has escape it’s home star system through collisions or interactions, or possibly a failed star.

Well, this is a problem. But there may be a solution. One of your physicists has theorized that the bender could be used to move planets through space. It’s time to test out her theory. There are several rogue planets within range of the bender.
